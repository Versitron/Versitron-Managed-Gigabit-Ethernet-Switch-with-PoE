## Why Choose a Managed Ethernet Switch

A **managed Ethernet switch** provides advanced control over your network that unmanaged switches cannot. Key advantages include:

- VLAN segmentation to isolate traffic and enhance security  
- Quality of Service (QoS) to prioritize critical business traffic  
- SNMP, web, and CLI management for monitoring and configuration  
- Bandwidth control, redundancy, and network diagnostics  
- PoE/PoE+ support to power connected devices directly  

Managed switches are essential for medium-to-large businesses, industrial networks, and CCTV deployments where uptime, monitoring, and control are critical.
